ABOUT

Eric is a first-year MA student in Social and Personality Psychology under the supervision of Dr. Amy Muise. He completed his BSc in Psychology and Biology at the University of Toronto – Mississauga. His previous research has explored an online speed dating paradigm and the role of personal and relational self-expansion in couples that have recently relocated. Broadly, Eric’s research interests include processes involved in the initiation of romantic relationships and ways in which couples can maintain their relationship and sexual satisfaction over time. Aside from research, Eric enjoys bouldering, board game nights, bowling, and watching/playing basketball.
